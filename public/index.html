How it works
1
Submit a URL — enter a seed URL and optional search terms, accept the TOS, and click Start.
2
Crawl runs — the crawler follows links from your seed URL through Tor, up to 50 pages. You get a job link to track progress.
3
Get results — when done, view matched pages and download results as a compressed JSON file.
